  • 2.1 We may process the following categories of personal data:

    • Enquiry data: information submitted through contact forms or service inquiries.

  • 2.2 Sources: Usage data may be collected through tools such as Google Analytics, LinkedIn Insights, or similar platforms.
  • 2.3 Purposes: We process personal data for:
    • Providing consulting services and responding to inquiries.
    • Sending newsletters, alerts, and service updates.
    • Analyzing site use to improve content and user experience.
    • Maintaining records of client and prospect communications.
  • 2.4 Legal basis: Processing is based on either (a) your consent, (b) performance of a contract, (c) compliance with legal obligations, or (d) legitimate interests such as marketing and service improvement.
